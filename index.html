What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking, a vital component of cybersecurity, involves identifying and fixing vulnerabilities in computer systems and networks to protect them from malicious attacks. Ethical hackers are authorized professionals who help organizations improve their security.

Techniques & Tools

  • Penetration Testing: Simulating attacks to find vulnerabilities.
  • Wireshark: A network protocol analyzer for monitoring traffic.
  • Metasploit: A tool for developing and testing exploits.
